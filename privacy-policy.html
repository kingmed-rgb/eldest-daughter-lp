Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how The Eldest Daughter Project collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our website or purchase digital products from us.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, purchase details, and messages you send to us. Payment information is processed by Stripe and is not stored by us.

How We Use Information

  • To provide digital products and customer support.
  • To process purchases and deliver order confirmations.
  • To improve our website, products, and user experience.
  • To send relevant updates or marketing emails when permitted by law.

Disclosure of Information

We may share information with service providers who help operate the website, process payments, deliver emails, host files, analyze traffic, or provide customer support. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

Payment Processing

Payments are handled by Stripe. Stripe may collect payment card details, billing information, fraud prevention data, and transaction information according to Stripe's own privacy practices.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Your Choices

You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link in those emails.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at eldestdaughterbook@gmail.com.
